About Hubspot

Hubspot is the leading Inbound Marketing hub in the world and they provide total solutions for Inbound Marketing and certify many classes of professionals that work in the Digital Marketing space.

The Hubspot tool is an all-in-one marketing and sales software that is used for SEO, Social Media, Analytics, Content Marketing, Display Advertising, CRM, Email Marketing, and Website Management.

Inbound Marketing

Inbound marketing is a digital marketing approach focused on attracting a targeted audience and helping them through the buying process using websites, search engines, emails, blogs, and social media. It is a very important aspect of converting leads to clients in a fast-paced digital environment and has been proven to work better than intrusive advertising.

World wide, the Inbound Marketing budget continues to grow and many forward-looking businesses are allocating more of their annual marketing spend on creating and expanding their presence through the Inbound Marketing niche.

“There is a lot to developing a successful Inbound Marketing campaign,” says Agbons Igiewe. “One thing to tell first-time clients is ‘learn to listen to your target market first, then utilise the right tools and techniques to reach them’.”
